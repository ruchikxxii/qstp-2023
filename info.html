“ There is always that one summer that changes you. ”


Quark Summer Technical Projects (QSTP) in association with CN (Coding Ninjas), GDG (Google Developer Group) & WTM (Women Techmakers) Goa , is back in its 9th edition with a total of 24 projects spanning varied areas of study. For the first time, QSTP is offering projects in fields like Game Development, Data Science, Product Management among others. Being conducted in a completely online mode, QSTP offers a great opportunity to learn and interact with the experienced mentors from BITS Pilani, Goa at the convenience of one's own.


1. QSTP is Open for participation for Students from any College across the Nation irrespective of their year of study and all projects are Free of Cost.

2. Completion of the project/course will award the participant with a 'Certificate of Completion'.Moreover, the top participants in each course shall be awarded with a 'Certificate of Excellence'. Certificates will be issued by Quark 2023 in association with CN, GDG & WTM Goa and certified by a BITS faculty member.

3. Last Date of Registration for the Projects is 9th July, 2022(11:59 PM IST).
